Daisy Sparkle

Add to Cart

  • Starting from the fragrant outermost layer of coconut mousse, layering fresh coconut cake with lightly sour passion fruit cream, adding a layer of hand-cooked yellow Cat Chu mango jam - this has always been LaFuong Pastry's most popular cake flavor. When you eat it, you will taste the fresh tropical fruit, mixed with a very pleasant sweet and sour taste. Designed with a lovely crown, embellished with Daisy flowers on a shiny bright yellow glaze background. If you have never tried the cake at LaFuong, this should be your first taste.
