  • Lily's Valley

    Lily's Valley has a sweet, lovely and very familiar flavor. The fragrant vanilla mousse layer made from Madagascar vanilla beans is the main theme of this cake. You will feel like eating a cool vanilla ice cream with a layer of hand-cooked jam from cherries and strawberries - fragrant and succulent. The highlight of the cake is also the crunchy almond crumble layer and the fresh green lemon peel cake layer. With a shape made from lovely flowers arranged on a pink mirror-coated background, Lily's Valley is a cake that is both delicious and pretty for you to easily choose.

  • Orient Scent

    A cake with a minimalist, elegant design, but containing a profound meaning. Orient Scent cake was born with the message of fullness & goodness. "Round Heaven - Square Earth" is as simple as that, created on a cool green mirrored background with a square shape representing the earth & a circle symbolizing the sky. The main flavors are fresh mango and pandan leaves - a bold Asian flavor, combined with European cream cheese, this cake will bring a fresh, fragrant and sweet feeling when enjoyed.

Get to know us

Jushalie Pastry has been in the baking business since 1992 providing small town's bakery expeditions for 20 years. We keep our store small to minimize our impact on the areas that are far from us and to ensure the other local bakery stores are not stressed or overwhelmed. Our store is a tight knit group of expert Chefs in the field of baking. All of our employees are passionate about creating and serving. We are all professional bakers and experts at pastries, so we can give you lots of information, and believe me, we have a lot to share. Meet the team
