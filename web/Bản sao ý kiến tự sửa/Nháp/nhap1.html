  • Lily's Valley

    Lily's Valley has a sweet, lovely and very familiar flavor. The fragrant vanilla mousse layer made from Madagascar vanilla beans is the main theme of this cake. You will feel like eating a cool vanilla ice cream with a layer of hand-cooked jam from cherries and strawberries - fragrant and succulent. The highlight of the cake is also the crunchy almond crumble layer and the fresh green lemon peel cake layer. With a shape made from lovely flowers arranged on a pink mirror-coated background, Lily's Valley is a cake that is both delicious and pretty for you to easily choose.

  • Daisy Sparkle

    Starting from the fragrant outermost layer of coconut mousse, layering fresh coconut cake with lightly sour passion fruit cream, adding a layer of hand-cooked yellow Cat Chu mango jam - this has always been LaFuong Pastry's most popular cake flavor. When you eat it, you will taste the fresh tropical fruit, mixed with a very pleasant sweet and sour taste. Designed with a lovely crown, embellished with Daisy flowers on a shiny bright yellow glaze background. If you have never tried the cake at LaFuong, this should be your first taste.

  • Be in Blosssom

    Brings a great balance between mild sourness, sweetness and tanginess in one bite by using lychee, coconut and raspberries. It can be said that this is a very interesting combination between Asia and Europe. Not only is it special in flavor, Be in Blossom's cake layer also has two different textures: the soft, spongy feeling coming from the Coconut Sponge layer and a bit of crunchiness from fresh coconut fibers in the Dacquoise layer. This is not only a great gift, but also a worthy choice for any birthday party, meeting with friends or family gatherings.

  • Beloved Darling

    If you're a fan of chocolate, try Beloved Darling - a mousse made from high-quality dark chocolate, topped with hand-cooked raspberry jam and a crémeux made from Madagascar vanilla beans. . Just like the explosion of flavor, the cake's appearance is decorated with a red mirror coating combined with a luxurious bronze-coated feather. This will be the perfect choice for all your parties.

  • Orient Scent

    A cake with a minimalist, elegant design, but containing a profound meaning. Orient Scent cake was born with the message of fullness & goodness. "Round Heaven - Square Earth" is as simple as that, created on a cool green mirrored background with a square shape representing the earth & a circle symbolizing the sky. The main flavors are fresh mango and pandan leaves - a bold Asian flavor, combined with European cream cheese, this cake will bring a fresh, fragrant and sweet feeling when enjoyed.

  • Whisper White

    The first note you will feel will be the fresh taste of the slow-cooked red orange jam. Next is the gentle sweetness of a layer of crémeux made from pure dark chocolate and the lingering aftertaste will be the richness of a layer of mousse made from Earlgrey tea that has been cooled overnight. With an elegant white mirror-coated appearance, adorned with delicate falling feathers, Whisper White feels neutral enough that you can comfortably give it to your loved one whether he or she is male or female.
